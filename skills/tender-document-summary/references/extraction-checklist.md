# Tender Extraction Checklist

Use this checklist when the tender package is large or high stakes.

## Identity

- Tender title
- Issuer or contracting authority
- Tender reference number
- Country, region, or delivery location
- Procurement method or lot structure

## Commercials

- Estimated value or budget
- Currency
- Contract duration and extension options
- Payment terms
- Pricing format and cost schedule requirements

## Timeline

- Publication date
- Clarification deadline
- Submission deadline
- Opening date
- Evaluation period
- Expected award or mobilization date

## Eligibility

- Registration or incorporation requirements
- Tax clearance or statutory compliance
- Required licenses
- Minimum turnover or audited accounts
- Similar-project experience thresholds
- Key staff qualifications

## Submission pack

- Technical proposal
- Financial proposal
- Company profile
- References or past performance evidence
- CVs or staffing plan
- Signed forms, declarations, or bid security

## Evaluation

- Technical criteria
- Financial criteria
- Weightings
- Mandatory pass or fail requirements
- Reserved rights of the issuer

## Legal and delivery risk

- Liability caps or unlimited liability
- Liquidated damages or penalties
- Insurance requirements
- Delivery timeline feasibility
- Acceptance criteria
- Ambiguous or conflicting instructions

## Recommendation logic

Recommend `Bid` only if:

- mandatory eligibility appears achievable,
- submission package is feasible before deadline,
- key risks are acceptable or controllable,
- and the opportunity fits the company's capability and likely win profile.
