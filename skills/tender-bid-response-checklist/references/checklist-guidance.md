# Checklist Guidance

## Task conversion rules

- Convert each mandatory submission requirement into at least one explicit task.
- Split compound work into separate tasks by owner role.
- Keep tasks measurable and auditable.

## Owner mapping defaults

- Bid strategy and package governance -> Bid Manager
- Technical method statement and architecture -> Technical Lead
- Commercial proposal and pricing -> Finance or Commercial Lead
- Contract terms and declarations -> Legal
- Team CVs and staffing matrix -> HR or Delivery Lead
- Final packaging and portal upload -> Bid Coordinator

## Dependency examples

- Pricing finalization depends on scope assumptions being approved.
- Final submission depends on legal and compliance sign-off.
- CV package depends on role allocation being confirmed.

## Readiness scoring

- `Ready`: all mandatory artifacts have owners, due dates, and no critical blockers.
- `At risk`: at least one critical artifact is incomplete or timeline is tight.
- `Not ready`: mandatory evidence is missing with no feasible recovery plan.

## Minimum quality bar

- At least one owner and due date for each critical artifact.
- At least one mitigation per critical risk.
- At least one explicit pre-submission gate.
